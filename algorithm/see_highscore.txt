See highest score
------------

Step 1: Open "highscore.txt" for reading
Step 2: Check to see if highscore.txt has any content
	-if it has no content:
		1. display a message saying there is no high score
		2. Skip step 3 and 4
Step 3: Read name and score from highscore.txt into variables
Step 4: Display name and score to the screen
